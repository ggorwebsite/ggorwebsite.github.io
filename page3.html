THE RATING SYSTEM

Below are the main rating groups used to define the optimization rating of each game reviewed by us.

A+ Optimization

Only given to games that have achieved excellent performance in any setting we have tested. This is rare reward that only the developers that put effort can achieve

A Optimization

Games that have achieved near perfect optimization, making sure most of todays computers are well prepared to handle the game

B Optimization

Games with good optimization. With some flaws in the optimization but it has been shown the game has recieved a decent amount of optimization

C Optimization

Games with decent optimization. These video games have shown minor optimization improvements. With some minor tweaks this could be improved but is enough to warrant a decent score

D Optimization

Games with little to no optimization. Unlike other games, the ones given this score have proven that there has not been any effort put into optimizing the game, or optimized to a barely negligible degree.

F Optimization

Games with no optimization and bad performance. The games have not been optimized and even show some signs of slowdowns, bad and flawed performance.
